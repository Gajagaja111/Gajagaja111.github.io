Quick Answers

FAQ

Common questions answered. Still have one? Just message me.

How long does a typical edit take?+
Reels take 1–2 days. YouTube videos take 3–5 days. Short films and brand packages take 7–14 days. Rush delivery is available on request.
How many revisions do I get?+
Unlimited revisions until you're 100% happy. I don't charge extra for changes — your satisfaction is the goal.
What footage formats do you accept?+
I accept all common formats — MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, RAW, and more. You can send files via Google Drive, WeTransfer, or any cloud link.
Do you work with international clients?+
Yes! I work with clients worldwide. Communication is via WhatsApp or email, and files are shared through cloud links. Payment via PayPal or bank transfer.
What software do you use?+
Primarily Adobe Premiere Pro and After Effects for editing and motion graphics. DaVinci Resolve for color grading. Adobe Audition for sound design.
Do you offer monthly retainer packages?+
Yes! If you need consistent editing every month, I offer discounted retainer rates. Contact me to discuss a custom package for your needs.
